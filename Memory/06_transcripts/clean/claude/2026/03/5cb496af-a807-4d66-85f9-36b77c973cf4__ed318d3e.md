### 6. **Platform-Specific Behavior to Document**

The `toPosixPath()` function is **platform-agnostic** because it:
- Always converts backslashes to forward slashes (works on any platform)
- Calls `normalize()` after slash conversion (handles dotting segments)
- Applies `replace(/\\/g, '/')` final pass (ensures no backslashes remain)

However, `normalize()` **is platform-aware** internally, so the behavior can subtly differ:
- On Windows: `normalize('C:\\foo')` → `C:\\foo`
- On Unix: `normalize('C:\\foo')` → `C:/foo` (after slash conversion)
